# 🤖 SkillVerse Bot Commands

## General

/start
- Starts the SkillVerse bot.

/profile
- Shows your Warrior profile.

/play
- Opens the SkillVerse Mini App.

/leaderboard
- Shows current rankings.

/wallet
- Displays MIM and vUSDT balances.

/invite
- Displays your referral link.

/halloffame
- Opens the Hall of Fame.

/news
- Shows the latest SkillVerse announcements.

/help
- Displays help and support information.
