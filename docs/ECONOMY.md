# 💰 SkillVerse Economy

## Currencies

### 🪙 MIM
The main in-game currency.

Earn MIM by:
- Winning matches
- Completing missions
- Daily rewards
- Referrals
- Seasonal events

MIM can be used for:
- Future cosmetics
- Special events
- Exclusive rewards

---

### 💎 vUSDT
Virtual USDT earned during Season 1.

Sources:
- Leaderboard rewards
- Referrals
- Community events

vUSDT is not withdrawable during Season 1.

At the end of Season 1:
- A reward pool will be announced.
- Eligible vUSDT may be converted into real USDT based on the season's rules and available funding.

---
