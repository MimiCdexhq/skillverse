# ⚔️ SkillVerse - Game Rules

## Game 1
Rock • Paper • Scissors

### Win Conditions
- Rock beats Scissors
- Scissors beats Paper
- Paper beats Rock

### Match Types
- Practice vs AI
- Casual PvP
- Ranked PvP

### Rewards
Win:
- +10 SkillScore
- +10 MIM

Draw:
- +2 SkillScore
- +2 MIM

Loss:
- +1 MIM (Participation Reward)

### Streaks
3 Wins = Bronze Streak
5 Wins = Silver Streak
10 Wins = Gold Streak

### Fair Play
- No bots
- No cheating
- Server decides match results
